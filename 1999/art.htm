I am an artist, which means that pretty much every day, I make something of no practical value. There is too much of it to put it all online, though I do keep some of it here in this cupboard, for superstitious reasons.

My most major recent project to make it online has been The N Testament, a page-a-day adaptation of the New English Bible. I also have a weekly strip, published on my other website, about, um, stuff.

To be honest, most of my art makes more sense to myself than to other people. For more explanation, read my manifestation.
