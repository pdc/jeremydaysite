The N Testament
30th October 1997

Poor and needy
needs the money
we will be guilty of sin
foreigners, orphans and widows
foreigners, orphans, and
a dispute
innocent, and the other one guilty

Duty to a sister (Elle)

(written) London hates me

(and I probably deserve it)
