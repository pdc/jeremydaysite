The N Testament
5th March 1999

Abandoned I
have forgotton the god who protects me like a mighty rock.

I order worship, idols tremble before him. But even the very morning would still be no again and again, would only be trouble and incurable pain.

(written) what's the point in shining if nobody's looking.
