The N Testament
30th June 1997

a reminder, like something tied on your forehead

a pillar of cloud

wandering

(written) whoops


Note: This was drawn about a week after a fire extinguisher fell on my head, the day I went back to the doctor. As it happens, I didn't have a broken neck, but the pain and concussiondominated the bible for some time after that.
