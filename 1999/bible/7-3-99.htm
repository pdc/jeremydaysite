The N Testament
7th March 1999

(I long for evening to come.....) but it has brought me nothing but terror.

This is a time of panic, defeat and confusion in the Valley of Vision.

Someone calls to me, how soon will the night be over? I answer, morning is coming, but night will come again. If you want to ask again, come back and ask.

What is happening? Why all of it on the roofs of the houses? The whole city is an uproar, filled with noise and excitement.
