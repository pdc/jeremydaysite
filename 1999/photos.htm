I take a lot of photos, mostly with my rubbish cameras. The rubbish cameras are poor quality, disposable, toy, second-hand; I even own two rubbish digital cameras. The cheapest was free, but most cost me less than the price of developing the film. And what pictures I take! Scruffy, scabby, and stuipid. I also, sometimes, borrow cameras, to photograph the things I love. See some photos by me.

My friends, especially one in particular, have lovely cameras, ranging from antique medium-format beauties to shiny tiny digitals, and quite a few of them have taken photos of me, probably because I sit still and don't complain. See some photos of me.
